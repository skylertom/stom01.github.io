Supreme Court Decisions:

A toolset for user driven analysis of Supreme Court opinions, votes and issues. George Brown and I used D3 to visualize justice voting blocks, geographical importance of issues, citation graphs, and analyze language data. We obtained data from scdb.wustl.edu in addition to scraping FindLaw.com for citation information.

Interactive Grade Projection and Calculation

A quick project I did while procrastinating finals because there were no interactive grade calculators. Takes input of assignments/test and returns slider bars for each and a projected grade.
